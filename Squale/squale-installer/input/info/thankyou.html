Thank you for trying Squale!

Now that Squale is installed, here are the main hints for you to play with it:

  • Click on the "Start Squale" shortcut to launch Squale,
  • Click on the "Open Squale Web Portal" shortcut to open your web browser and log into Squale Web Portal,
    • To connect as administrator, use squaleadmin / admin,
    • To connect as a simple user, use user1 / user1 or user2 / user2,
  • When you want to stop, click on the "Stop Squale" shortcut.
For any other information, please visit Squale official's web site, and more precisely its user documentation.

And now... Enjoy! :-)
